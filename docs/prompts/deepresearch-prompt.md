# DeepResearch Prompt: Premium Landing Page Design Research

## Context

I am building a premium landing page for a personal transformation/coaching program called "Happiness". The target audience is successful entrepreneurs and executives (primarily men 35-50) who have achieved financial success but feel inner emptiness or stagnation.

**Design Concept:** "Imagine we're designing a website for a Museum of Modern Art that launched a psychological support program for millionaires. It should be strict and minimalist, but with bold art elements inside."

**Tech Stack:** Next.js 14, React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, GSAP with ScrollTrigger

---

## Research Tasks

Please conduct comprehensive research on the following 4 topics and provide detailed findings with specific examples, links, and actionable recommendations.

---

### TOPIC 1: Premium Hero Sections with Art Elements

**What I need:**
Find 5-7 exceptional examples of Hero sections from premium landing pages (coaches, psychologists, luxury brands, art galleries) that feature:

- Unique text animations (staggered reveals, character-by-character, morphing)
- Integration of photography with graphic/art elements (brush strokes, abstract shapes, paint splashes)
- Brush stroke or paint effect overlays on images or backgrounds
- Scroll-triggered animations that enhance storytelling
- Premium typography with serif fonts for headlines

**For each example provide:**
1. Website URL
2. Screenshot description or key visual elements
3. What makes it exceptional
4. Technical approach (if identifiable: GSAP, Framer Motion, CSS, etc.)
5. How the art elements are integrated with content

**Specific interest:**
- How to overlay semi-transparent brush strokes on hero images
- Text reveal animations that feel premium, not generic
- Parallax effects between photo and graphic elements
